Author's Note

This dictionary app is created with the vision of preserving and promoting the Vaiphei language. It is designed as a living resource: words and meanings can be added over time, making it ever-growing. The goal is to support students, researchers, and community members who want to learn, translate, or deepen their knowledge of Vaiphei and English. Accuracy, clarity, and accessibility are at the heart of this project.
